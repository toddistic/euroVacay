This is the public-safe version of the trip plan.

## Public-Safe Trip Shape

- Arrival area: gentle recovery, castle visit, riverside walking, easy food.
- Southern countryside: prehistoric sites, cathedral towns, Georgian architecture,
  historic villages, and possible castle dining.
- Yorkshire: medieval York, moors/coast, estate activities, abbey ruins, Dales scenery,
  and a possible guided gorge-walking activity.
- London: museums, theatre, markets, royal history, food, and flexible split activities.

## Planning Priorities

- Keep one major experience per day where possible.
- Build in calm evenings.
- Favor booked or structured activities only when they meaningfully improve the day.
- Keep dietary notes private unless they are generic venue checks.
- Keep exact dates, times, lodging, and travel movements out of public files.
